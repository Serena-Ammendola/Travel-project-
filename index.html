Why Naples?

Naples, the city of a thousand colors

Naples is famous for multiple aspects that make it a unique and fascinating city, first and foremost for its history and culture.Founded by the Greeks in the 18th century B.C., it boasts a history of thousands of years with a succession of dominations leaving a priceless legacy of monuments, works of art and traditions. Its historic center, the largest in Europe, has been declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Naples is famous for its pizza and gastronomy, it is famous for its landscapes and nature, for lying at the foot of Vesuvius, an active volcano, but its geographical location makes it an ideal point for exploring both the Sorrento coast and the Gulf Islands. Naples is famous for its Neapolitans, known for their hospitality, warmth and liveliness. Pizza

My favorite places to eat pizza in Naples

Pizzeria Donna Sofia Ai Tribunali

In the heart of Naples, you can taste here an amazing a traditional pizza, fried pizza and street food while waiting to get your table.

Address:

Via dei Tribunali, 89, 80138 Napoli NA, Italia

What I like about it

In this restaurant you will feel like home. On the wall you will find a lot of Naples history. The food is amazing and it is a perfect stop after a walk in the heart of the city!

L'Antica Pizzeria Da Michele

Da Michele is the oldest pizzeria born in Naples. Here you have only few options but you will definitely try the traditional pizza.

Address:

Via Cesare Sersale, 1, 80139 Napoli NA, Italia

What I like about it

From the 1870 this pizzeria has still a lot of success. Do you remember the movie Eat Pray Love? Yes, Julia Roberts was here! Just be prepared, you have to wait a lot before try it!
